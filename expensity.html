Expenses Tracking App

Mobile, App

Tracking of business expenses for employees - Android and iOS

This app is another attempt to showcase how capable is NativeScript technology in developing for enterprise scenarios. The goal was to demonstrate that it creates really native apps, with respect to the platform specific user experience (Android vs iOS), out of the box, without the platform specific code.

Forms and input
Primary and secondary actions
Multi-level navigation
All-in-one document for information architecture/content structure/interaction design (LeanUX FTW)
