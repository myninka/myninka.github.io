Field Services App

Mobile, App

Service assets maintenance app for iOS and Android

The app demonstrates how NativeScript technology can be used to develop enterprise mobile apps for field services. The idea was to create a hybrid app and then rework it with NativeScript giving the potential customers the ability to share code between web and native applications, which was the selling point. Started as a hybrid app, the UI incorporated patterns from iOS and Android, as best solutions for interaction problem.

Wireframes

The UX process followed a very lean fashion. The developers started the implementation based on this basic IA document I provided; after that they received the wireframes; and finally the polished UI.

Each of the deliverables was first discussed with the team before it became final.
