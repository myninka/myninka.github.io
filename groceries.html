Groceries for iOS

This app is designed for the NativeScript getting started tutorial. The purpose is to showcase NativeScript technology on a level understood by novice users in a meaningful scenario. It had to combine essential elements from the technology, and also animations and transitions which was its biggest flaw in the begining. It is simple two screens app, but the user tests, we ran, discovered it is too complicated for novice NativeScript developers, along with few other product specific flaws in the development process.

Storyboard for the transitions and animations

In the lean UX process which I followed, it was more convinient to make storyboards of the animations instead of exact animations.

The loading animation was the only exception.
