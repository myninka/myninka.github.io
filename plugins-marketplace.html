Plugins Marketplace web app

The goal of this web application is to provide NativeScript developer with an easy way to discover and publish plugins, from a single supported by the community place. The plugins needed to be easy to discover, opened to community feedback and rating.

I decided on the maximum layout flexibility because of the uncurated content and different form factors, and minimum visual clutter using the NativeScript brand identifiers and making this community driven website a seamless part of the NativeScript ecosystem.
