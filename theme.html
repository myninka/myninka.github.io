The task was to design a theme which will be the default visual design applied in the NativeScript apps. A NativeScript app is a cross-platform app by definition (for iOS and Android) and I wanted to be maximum close to the native look and feel of each platform - I didnâ€™t want the apps to lose their native UI, as opposed to a hybrid app which look the same on each platform. I created the theme with this in mind -  it has iOS look for iOS and Material design for Android. Also there are light and dark versions, along with several color skins with primary and secondary accent colors predefined.
